What is Olymp Trade?

Trading futures with Olymp Trade is one of the most lucrative and therefore popular ways to make money online. Fixed time trades are a derivative instrument that allows you to speculate on the likely price movements of financial assets.

Essentially, fixed time trades allow you to ‘bet’ or ‘wager’ (based on careful analysis) on the direction of movement of assets such as stocks, cryptocurrencies, commodities and, of course, forex. In this case, you are not interested in the underlying assets. Instead, you are simply interested in the directions in which their prices are likely to move.
